Team Calmadoro
Calmadoro is a Pomodoro timer that plays you calm music while you work.
The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. The idea is to use time to motivate yourself, eliminate burnout, manage distractions, reduce back pain and generally to maintain a healthy work-life balance by not getting sucked into your computer screen for an indefinite amount of time.
